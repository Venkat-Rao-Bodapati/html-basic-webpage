Delicious Recipe:
Chicken Biryani

This is one of my favourite dishes! It,s flavourful,
aromatic,and perfect for family gatherings.


Ingredients

  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken
  • Spices & Herbs
  • Yogurt
  • Onions & Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice.
  2. Cook chicken with spices.
  3. Layer rice and chicken
  4. Steam cook for 20 minutes
